🌴✨ The Unity Agenda

💖 A Peaceful Plan for a Better Florida State RP 💖

  • Safer Streets
  • Stronger Communities
  • Jobs Over Jail
  • Mental Health
  • Honest Leadership

🛡️ 1. Safer Streets

  • 👮‍♂️ Friendly community policing
  • 🏋️‍♀️ Officers trained to calm situations
  • 👶 Helping youth before they turn to crime

🏡 2. Stronger Communities

  • 👣 Weekly town talks (everyone’s voice matters!)
  • 🍔 Chill events: BBQs, car meets, fun hangouts
  • 🌟 Local reps to help with neighborhood issues

💼 3. Jobs Over Jail

  • 🛠️ Support small biz & second chances
  • 🎓 Trade school help for real careers
  • 👨‍💼 Internships in public service

💖 4. Mental Health Support

  • 🚑 Crisis teams with trained helpers
  • 📞 In-game helpline for safe chats
  • 🌿 Recovery & wellness centers (not jail!)

🗾️ 5. Honest Leadership

  • 💸 Open budgets so everyone sees the plan
  • 🚫 No corruption – clean and fair gov
  • 💬 Fair hiring for all public roles

# Our Slogan! - Unite the State, End the Hate!
